Three Things

  1. Willingness to let go of your narrative of your storyline
  2. Be willing, in this moment, to let go of your spiritual history, your spiritual catalog, understandings, techniques, etc.
  3. Get a one-pointed approach. When you let go of the narrative, then what do you cling to? It comes down to a laser-focus on the mind. Everything else gets very quiet like it doesn't exist. Stay with that focus and alertness and forego every thought and narrative.
